Berlin Tempelhof Airport Virtual Fieldtrip

Introduction

The former Berlin Tempelhof Airport (formerly with airport code THF), in its decomissioning, provided Berlin with a large availability of land for new uses in the core of a large urban area. In our strongly capitalist society land, especially in urban cores, is highly valued and sought after for development. However, locals interests have had a strong influence in shaping how the land has been reallocated so far. This site provides a unique case study for the analysis of different populations' needs and desires as well as economic and political interestes in an urban setting of increasing popularity and cost. ... etc ...
