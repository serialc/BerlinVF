Storymaps

Storymaps provides a simple and easy way to students to build stories and data together to showcase results as a map. Examples may include historical site information, urban planning, community garden, urban housing challenges, land-use planning, contemporary urban geography challenges or issues, or infrastructure access for cyclists (bike lanes) or pedestrians.
